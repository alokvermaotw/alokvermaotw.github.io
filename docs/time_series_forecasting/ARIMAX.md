# Assumptions
1. The time series involved are weakly stationary or integrated of some order.
2. All observed time series are combinations of white noises only, and perhaps a constant.
3. There are no known/suspected predictor variables.
4. There are no level shifts.
5. There are no deterministic time trends of the form 1, 2, 3,…, t.
6. There are no seasonal dummies.
7. There are no one-time anomalies.
8. The model parameters are constant over time.
9. The error process is homoscedastic (constant) over time.
