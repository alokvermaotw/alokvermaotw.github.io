- Quantile Quantile Plots
- Visual representation of how two datasets compare in terms of their distribution 
- Used to check whether or not a dataset fits a certain theoretical distribution.
- Steps:
	- Standardize your data = (X-mean)/sd
		- Compute Quantiles
		- Compare to gaussian quantiles
