# Facets of NLP

## Sentiment Analysis
- Measures the sentiment of the text given word or sentence.
- -1 means Highly Negative
- 0 means Neutral
- +1 means Highly Positive


## Topic Modeling
### LDA
Latent Dirichlet Allocation
- It is a generative probability model means it is tend to provide a model for the distribution of outputs and inputs based on _latent variables_.
- _Latent Variables_: Variables that are not directly observed but inferred from other variables from the training dataset. 

### NTM
Neural Topic Model
