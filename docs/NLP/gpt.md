After [[ELMo]], In Later 2018, a research team led by Alex Redford at Open AI released GPT to improve language understanding by generative pre-training. 
- GPT is based on the [[transformers]] architecture but performs two training steps. 
	- GPT learns a language model from a large unlabeled text corpus,
	- GPT performs a supervised learning step with labeled data to learn a specific NLP tasks such as text classification. 

- GPT is only trained and can predict context from left to right, which is often referred to as uni-directional.
